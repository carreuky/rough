Taking part in a fundraising event.

Are you a keen golfer, an avid walker, long distance runner or sky diving thrill seeker? There is a huge variety of events you can take part in to raise money for us but mostly importantly to have fun or challenge yourself! Every September we host our Step-tember Challenge. Walk, run, cycle or move 50km/30 miles across September and ask friends and family to sponsor you. The money raised will help young people in Kenya to step-into education during back-to-school season. See our news page for the latest updates on the upcoming challenge.
